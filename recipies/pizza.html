About Pizza

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a flat, round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like vegetables, meats, or herbs, baked until golden and bubbly. It's a versatile, globally enjoyed meal perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Cooked meat
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Combine flour, salt, and olive oil in a mixing bowl. Gradually add the yeast mixture and knead into a smooth, elastic dough (about 8-10 minutes).
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise for 1-2 hours, until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into desired portions. Roll each portion into a circle or your preferred pizza shape.
  6. Place the rolled dough on a pizza stone or baking tray.
  7. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base.
  8. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese and add your choice of toppings (vegetables, meats, etc.).
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.
  10. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with dried oregano, and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  11. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
