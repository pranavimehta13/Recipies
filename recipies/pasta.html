About Pasta

Pasta is a versatile Italian staple made from wheat flour and water, sometimes with eggs, shaped into various forms like spaghetti, penne, or fusilli. It can be served with sauces, vegetables, or proteins, making it a global favorite for its simplicity and adaptability.

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Red chili flakes
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Fresh parsley or basil (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add salt generously (like seawater).
  2. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente (firm but cooked).
  3. Drain the pasta, reserving ½ cup of pasta water for the sauce. Set aside.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté garlic and onions until fragrant and translucent.
  5. Add pureed or diced tomatoes and tomato pasteStir well.
    6. .
  6. Season with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, and red chili flakes if desired.
  7. Simmer for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  8. Add reserved pasta water to adjust the sauce's consistency, if needed.
  9. Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce, ensuring it is well coated.
  10. Let the pasta cook in the sauce for 1-2 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  11. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  12. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley and a drizzle of olive oil, if desired.
